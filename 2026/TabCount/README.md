# TabCount

This is a small Safari extension I made for myself. It tells you (well, me,
since I'm not distributing it) how many tabs you (I) have open.

As I'm writing this in 2026, it's been serving me well for over 7 years, but I
just realized I never pushed it anywhere. So, doing that now.

I've also not touched it over those 7 years, and it's in need of a touch-up, as
it keeps showing me 0 when I definitely have multiple tabs open. Like,
hundreds, overs dozens of windows. (That last part may be part of the issue.)
